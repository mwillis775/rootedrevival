WHO WE ARE

A family business that builds growing systems — from backyard gardens to modern greenhouses — because we believe the world needs more people growing things, and fewer reasons not to.

// The Short Version

Rooted Revival started because landscapes should give back more than they take. We saw a disconnect between how people treat their yards and what the land actually needs — and between what modern agriculture promises and what it actually delivers.

We're not here to sell you the most expensive option. We're here because we know how to grow things, we know how to build things, and we believe that combining tradition with the best of modern technology is how you get results that last.

Every project — whether it's a small herb garden or a full-scale greenhouse — is a chance to put something good into the ground and help it thrive.

// Why We Do It

We don't believe money is what makes people happy. Family, faith, and friendship are what matter. But we're honest about the fact that we're a business — we have a profit motive, and it's to provide for our family. That clarity keeps us grounded and keeps us honest with our customers.

// What We Believe

Principles

🌍

NATURE WORKS TOGETHER

Monoculture is a dead end. Healthy ecosystems are diverse — plants, fungi, insects, microbes — all working in concert. We design systems that mirror this, because it's what actually works long-term.

🧪

TRADITION MEETS TECHNOLOGY

We don't pick sides. In-ground growing has millennia of wisdom behind it. Aeroponics has decades of data. The best results come from knowing when to use which — and having the skill to build both.

🌾

AGAINST SYNTHETIC NITROGEN

We're against enriched nitrogen fertilizers — the Haber-Bosch process that pulls nitrogen from air. It destroys soil biology, pollutes waterways, and creates dependency. Healthy soil feeds itself.

🧬

PRO SCIENCE, PRO ACCESS

We believe genetic science can benefit all of humanity. What we oppose is the patenting of life — the idea that a corporation can own a seed. The technology should serve everyone, not shareholders.

🤝

HONEST WORK

We won't recommend what you don't need. Our profit motive is simple: provide for our family by doing good work. That means telling you the truth, even when a simpler solution costs us money.

🌱

END THE MONOCULTURE

The homogenized lawn-and-chemical approach to land doesn't just fail ecologically — it fails people. We want every yard, garden, and farm to be a living, diverse, productive piece of earth.

// What Makes Us Different

Most landscaping companies plant things. Most greenhouse companies sell structures. Most tech companies write software. We do all of it, because a growing system is all of it.

When the person who writes your automation software also understands plant physiology, and the person pouring your greenhouse slab also designed the fertigation system — nothing falls through the cracks.

Where our expertise ends, our network of industry partners begins. We work with the best in every trade to deliver results we'd stake our name on.
