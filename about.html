WHAT IS OPENSCHOLAR/GRABNET?

We're building a distributed knowledge archive that belongs to everyone. No corporations. No algorithms. No bots. Just humans sharing knowledge on infrastructure we collectively own.

THE PROBLEM

The internet is broken. Knowledge is locked behind paywalls. Social media is overrun with AI slop and engagement farming. Your data is harvested and sold. Platforms can delete your work, ban your account, or disappear entirely.

We've handed control of human knowledge to corporations that optimize for profit, not preservation.

OUR SOLUTION

A distributed archive where every user runs a node. Content is stored across the network—no single point of failure. Your identity lives on your machine, not in a corporate database. Human moderators, not algorithms.

🌐

DISTRIBUTED BY DESIGN

When you download the app, you become part of the infrastructure. Together, we build an archive that no one can shut down.

💡 OUR VALUES

🔓

OPEN ACCESS

Knowledge should be free. No paywalls, no subscriptions, no artificial scarcity. If it's in the archive, anyone can access it.

🛡️

USER OWNERSHIP

Your account, your data, your node. We can't ban you because we don't control you. You are sovereign over your digital identity.

👥

HUMAN CURATION

No algorithms deciding what you see. Human moderators reviewing content. Community-driven organization and discovery.

🌍

RESILIENCE

Distributed architecture means no single point of failure. The more nodes, the stronger the network. We survive together.

🔒

PRIVACY

No tracking, no analytics, no data harvesting. We don't know who you are and we don't want to. Privacy is the default.

SUSTAINABILITY
