📋 UPLOAD GUIDELINES

✅ What We Accept

Research papers, educational videos, tutorials, original art, music, documentation, datasets. Anything that contributes knowledge.

🚫 What We Don't

AI-generated slop, SEO spam, copyrighted material you don't own, illegal content, harassment, or anything that wastes storage.

👀 Moderation

All uploads are reviewed by human moderators before being distributed to the network. Spam gets you banned.

♾️ Permanence

Content on IPFS is hard to remove once distributed. Think before you upload—this is meant to be an archive.
