WHAT WE BUILD

From a backyard garden bed to a fully automated modern greenhouse — and everything in between. If it grows, we know how to make it thrive.

🌿

// Growing Systems

We understand growing in every possible way — from in the ground to aeroponics — and more importantly, we know where each method actually works best. Tradition and cutting-edge technology aren't at odds. The best systems combine both.

In-Ground Gardens

Traditional beds, raised beds, hugelkultur, and no-till systems. Sometimes the old ways are the right ways. Soil-building that compounds year over year.

Food Forests

Multi-layered perennial ecosystems producing fruit, nuts, berries, herbs, and medicine. Self-sustaining after establishment.

Hydroponics & Aquaponics

Water-based growing — from simple deep water culture to integrated fish-and-plant systems. Year-round production in controlled environments.

Aeroponics

Mist-based root-zone delivery for maximum efficiency. Ideal for high-value crops and research environments where precision matters.

Native Plant Landscapes

Oklahoma-native plantings that support local pollinators and wildlife while thriving without irrigation or synthetic inputs.

Pollinator & Wildlife Habitat

Designed ecosystems that support bees, butterflies, birds, and beneficial insects. Biodiversity is the foundation of resilience.

🏗️

// Greenhouses & Controlled Environments

We design and build greenhouses from the ground up — that means the concrete, the framing, the glazing, the HVAC, the electrical, and the automation software. One team, one vision, no gaps between trades.

Greenhouse Design & Build

Custom structures from hobby-scale to commercial production. We handle everything from foundation to finish — concrete, steel, glazing, and fit-out.

Climate Control & HVAC

Heating, cooling, dehumidification, and ventilation engineered for plant health. Energy-efficient systems designed for your specific crops and climate zone.

Electrical & Lighting

Full electrical systems, supplemental grow lighting, and power management. LED spectrum tuning for optimized growth at every stage.

Automation & Controls

Sensors, controllers, and custom software for environment monitoring and automated fertigation, irrigation, and climate management.

Fertigation Systems

Precision nutrient delivery — from simple injector setups to fully automated dosing systems with EC/pH monitoring and adjustment.

Retrofit & Upgrades

Bring existing structures up to modern standards. Add automation, improve climate control, or convert a building into a growing space.

// Construction & Trades

Our projects don't stop at the plant. We do the sitework, the concrete, the framing, the plumbing, and the wiring. Working with industry leaders in every trade so nothing falls through the cracks.

Concrete & Foundations

Slabs, footings, retaining walls, and hardscape. Proper foundations for structures that last.

General Contracting

Full project management from permits to punch list. We coordinate the trades and keep your project on track.

Electrical

Service panels, circuits, lighting, and controls. Residential and agricultural electrical work.

Plumbing & Irrigation

Water supply, drainage, irrigation systems, and rainwater harvesting. Getting water where it needs to go efficiently.

Earthwork & Grading

Site preparation, drainage solutions, swale construction, and landscape grading for proper water management.

Structures & Fencing

Pergolas, arbors, raised bed frames, deer fencing, and other landscape structures built to last.

// Technology & Software

The same team that builds your greenhouse writes the software that runs it. Hardware design, firmware, networking, dashboards, and data — all in-house.

Environment Monitoring

Custom sensor networks for temperature, humidity, CO₂, light, soil moisture, and nutrient levels. Real-time data, historical trends.

Automation Software

Control systems for irrigation, climate, lighting, and fertigation. Scheduled or responsive — your environment runs itself.

Hardware & Electronics

PCB design, microcontroller programming, relay boards, and custom actuator integration. If it doesn't exist off the shelf, we build it.

Data & Analytics

Dashboards, alerting, and growth tracking. Turn sensor data into actionable insights for better yields and earlier problem detection.

🗺️

// Consultation & Design

Not every project needs a full build. Sometimes you just need the right plan, a second opinion, or someone who's done it before to walk you through the options.

Site Assessment

We study your land — soil, water, sun, microclimate, existing ecology — and tell you what's possible and what makes sense.

System Design

Complete growing system plans — from backyard beds to commercial operations. Species selection, layout, infrastructure, and phasing.

Soil & Water Analysis

Understanding what you're working with is the first step. We test, interpret, and recommend amendments and management strategies.

Project Planning

Budget estimation, material sourcing, scheduling, and phased implementation plans for projects of any scale.

// How We Work

Every project starts with listening. We want to understand what you're trying to grow, why it matters to you, and what your land can support.

We don't push products or upsell systems you don't need. If a simple in-ground garden does the job, that's what we'll build. If the project calls for a climate-controlled facility with automated fertigation, we can do that too.

We partner with industry leaders in every aspect of our work — from soil scientists to HVAC engineers — so you get the absolute best at every stage.
