// Core Features

Knowledge Without Gatekeepers

🔐

Censorship Resistant

Content is distributed across a decentralized network. No single entity can remove or suppress knowledge once published.

♾️

Permanent Storage

Published works are preserved indefinitely through content-addressed storage and network replication.

🔓

Open Access

No paywalls, no subscriptions. Knowledge should be freely accessible to everyone, everywhere.

✍️

Author Attribution

Cryptographic signatures ensure proper attribution. Your work remains yours with verifiable provenance.

🌐

Global Distribution

Content is served from the nearest available peer, ensuring fast access regardless of geography.

📊

Rich Metadata

Full support for citations, DOIs, ORCID IDs, and academic metadata standards.

// Technology

Powered by GrabNet

Open Scholar is built on GrabNet, a decentralized web hosting protocol developed by Rooted Revival. GrabNet provides:

  • ◆ Stable Addresses — Paper IDs never change, even after revisions
  • ◆ P2P Distribution — Content hosted across global network of peers
  • ◆ Cryptographic Signing — Ed25519 signatures prove authorship
  • ◆ Auto-Replication — Nodes automatically sync and preserve content
