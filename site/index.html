// Regenerative by Design

Traditional landscaping fights nature with chemicals, constant mowing, and thirsty non-native plants. We take a different approach.

Regenerative landscaping works with ecological systems to create landscapes that improve over time—building soil, supporting pollinators, capturing water, and reducing maintenance.

Your yard becomes part of the solution, not the problem.

// Services

What We Grow

🌳

Food Forests

Multi-layered edible ecosystems that produce fruit, nuts, berries, and herbs with minimal maintenance once established.

🌿

Native Plant Gardens

Oklahoma-native plantings that support local pollinators, birds, and wildlife while thriving without irrigation or chemicals.

💧

Rain Gardens & Bioswales

Capture and infiltrate stormwater on-site, reducing runoff and recharging groundwater with beautiful planted features.

🦋

Pollinator Habitat

Create certified wildlife habitat with native flowering plants that support bees, butterflies, and other crucial pollinators.

🏡

Lawn Conversion

Transform water-hungry turf grass into low-maintenance native meadows, groundcovers, or productive food gardens.

🪨

Ecological Restoration

Restore degraded land to healthy, functioning ecosystems through soil remediation and native species establishment.

// About

The Grower

Michael Willis

I started Rooted Revival because I believe landscapes should give back more than they take. After years of studying botany, permaculture, and ecological restoration, I saw a disconnect between how we treat our yards and what the land actually needs.

Every project is an opportunity to heal a small piece of earth—to build soil instead of depleting it, to support wildlife instead of poisoning it, and to grow food instead of just grass.

Based in Tulsa, I serve the greater Green Country region with design, installation, and consultation services.
